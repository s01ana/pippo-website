Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

  • What is PIPPO Token? 🤔

    PIPPO Token is a community-driven meme coin built on the Solana blockchain. Inspired by the beloved character from the Doraemon series, PIPPO combines the fun of memes with the innovative features of blockchain technology, aiming to create a vibrant ecosystem for its holders.

  • How do I buy PIPPO? 💎

    To purchase PIPPO, follow these steps:

    Create a Wallet: Download a Solana-compatible wallet, such as Phantom, from the app store or Google Play Store. For desktop users, install the Phantom extension from the official website.

    Acquire SOL: Ensure you have Solana (SOL) tokens in your wallet, as they are required for transactions on the Solana network. You can purchase SOL on major exchanges like Binance or Coinbase and transfer them to your wallet.

    Access a Decentralized Exchange (DEX): Navigate to a Solana-based DEX, such as Raydium, using your browser. Connect your wallet to the DEX platform.

    Swap SOL for PIPPO: On the DEX, locate the PIPPO token by entering its contract address. Ensure you have the correct token to avoid scams. Enter the amount of SOL you wish to swap for PIPPO and execute the transaction.

  • What are the tokenomics? 📊

    PIPPO operates with straightforward tokenomics: Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 PIPPO tokens Taxes: PIPPO is designed with 10% taxes for transactions, converted to USDC that will pay the PIPPO SQUAD (thats including you if you buy, sell or just do nothing) every 5 minutes!

  • Do You Burn? 🗑️

    NO BURN! We do not Burn Money! We reward people who have money to make more money!

  • How can I join the community? 👥

    We'd love for you to join the PIPPO Squad! Connect with us through our official channels: Telegram: https://t.me/pipposquad Engage with fellow PIPPO SQUAD, stay updated on the latest news, and be part of our vibrant and growing community!
